This trip report form is intended to provide a quick means for documenting facility visits. It includes the functionality to attach trip reports to Facility Performance Improvement Plans (FPIP) 

Completion Instructions:Select the facility visited from the drop down list.  Select your organization and name from the drop down list.Select the dates of your travel from the calendar.Select the main reason for your trip from the drop down list.Write a short synopsis of the results/anaysis of the trip (5000 character Max.).If you have a more indepth report or pictures to attach, use the attachment button to navigate to your document.  Please move pictures into a word or pdf document before uploading for security reasons. **USE a 1 word file name**. IE: "XXXASR.doc" not "XXX ASR trip report.doc"If you have addition pending actions, such as instructions for further work for the SSC or another group, add them to the actions list. Use the checkbox to add to the facility performance improvement plan (FPIP) if there are pending followup actions or to keep facility information with the FPIP.

Upon submittal, a copy of the trip report will be emailed to your immediate supervisor. If the FPIP checkbox has been selected, a notification will be sent to the facility FLM as well.

To search for a specific type of reports, use the second tab.  You can filter on facility type, facility ident, organization or employee.  Once you have filtered the results, you can use the web browser find function (CTRL-F) to search for keywords in the text of the reports.
